Typographic Hierarchy

A typographic hierarchy is a system for organizing type in a manner that helps the reader easily navigate content. Hierarchies are designed to feature content, to make it easy to find information, and to isolate There are several ways to establish a visual typographic hierarchy, including

  • Size
  • Weight
  • Color
  • Position
  • Type Contrast

Typographic Systems

There are more ways to organize type than just through establishing hierarchy. Although grid is undoubtedly one of the most popular systems, there are several other ways to organize text. Understanding different typographic systems allows you to play with systemic thinking. Structure is just as choosing typefaces in terms of communicating a message
