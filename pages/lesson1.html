Activity

Today in class we're going to be creating letterforms from scratch with everyone's favorite thing... food! Hopefully you brought some snacks to share.

Have fun experimenting with creating interesting, evocative, and legible letterforms while snacking and getting to know your new classmates! Take some pictures to document your delicious letters!

Homework

For homework, your task is to create a piece of handmade typography. Choose a word, think about what it represents and how you can create it using ordinary objects. Or go backwards, and choose your medium first, then figure out your word! Either way, be creative and have fun! Sky is the limit, but absolutely no hand-drawn or vectorized type.

Document your handmade typography project and upload it Behance. Remember to add this decal's Behance as a contributor.

Think up two questions you have about your work in order to get some feedback from your peers in our first homework critique session.

Midterm Project

Your midterm project will be to design a book, with a minimum of eight pages. This project is due March 17, which gives you six weeks. For this first week, try to come up with a concept of what your book will be about. You are welcome to write your own copy, or to use material that is free to use (check the copyright).

For some inspiration, check out:

  • Art History is Actually Hilarious by Laura Javier
  • The Vanishing Bee by Michelle Fong

Reading

  • Letter by Ellen Lupton

  • Everything I've Ever Learned About Giving Design Critiques I Learned From Tim Gunn by Dan Saffer
